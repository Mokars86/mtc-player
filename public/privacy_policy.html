Privacy Policy for MTc Player

Last updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to MTc Player ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

2. Data We Collect

We collect information that allows us to provide you with the best experience possible. This includes:

Personal Information

  • Account Data: When you register, we collect your email address, username, and password (encrypted) via Supabase Authentication.
  • User Content: We store posts, comments, and media files you voluntarily upload to the community feed or your profile.

Payment Information

We use Flutterwave for payment processing. We do not store your financial data (credit card numbers, banking details) on our servers. All payment transactions are processed securely through Flutterwave's encrypted gateway.

Device and Usage Data

We may automatically collect detailed information about your device, including device name, operating system version, and unique device identifiers (Android ID) to help us debug crashes and improve performance.

3. App Permissions

To provide its core functionality as a music player, MTc Player requires the following permissions:

  • Storage (READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / READ_MEDIA_AUDIO): Required to scan, list, and play music files located on your device. We do not upload your local music files to our servers.
  • Internet Access: Required to connect to our servers for user authentication, community features, and payment processing.

4. Third-Party Services

We may share information with third-party vendors who perform services for us or on our behalf:

  • Supabase: For backend database, authentication, and file storage services.
  • Flutterwave: To process secure payments.
  • Google Gemini: To provide AI-powered features and recommendations.

5. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You have the right to access, update, or delete the information we have on you.

Account Deletion: You can request the deletion of your account and all associated data directly within the App settings or by contacting us. Upon request, we will permanently remove your personal data from our systems.

6. Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information. While we have taken reasonable steps to secure the personal information you provide to us, please be aware that despite our efforts, no security measures are perfect or impenetrable, and no method of data transmission can be guaranteed against any interception or other type of misuse.
