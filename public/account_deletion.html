Data Deletion Request

At MTc Player, your privacy is important to us. In accordance with Google Play Store data safety policies, users have the right to request the deletion of their account and associated data.

Option 1: Delete Account via App

You can delete your account and data instantly through the application. This is the fastest way to remove your information.

  1. Open the MTc Player app.
  2. Go to Profile > Settings.
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm your choice. Your account and personal data will be permanently removed.

Option 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you are unable to access the app or prefer to request deletion manually, please contact our support team. We will process your request within 7 business days.

Please send an email with the subject "Data Deletion Request" and include your registered username or email address.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

Once your account is deleted, the following data will be permanently removed from our servers:

  • Personal Information: Username, email address, and profile details.
  • User Content: All posted feed items, comments, and uploaded media.
  • App Data: Saved preferences and playlists linked to your account.

Note: Some data might be retained for a short period solely for legal compliance or fraud prevention purposes, as outlined in our Privacy Policy.
